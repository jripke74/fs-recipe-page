Chocolate Chip Cookies

Thes cookies are a family favorite. They are made with the finest ingredients. Milk chocolate chips are used allong with organic butter, flour, vanilla, and sugar. They literally melt in your mouth. You can't just have one.

Ingredients

  • 2 1/2 cups organic flour
  • 1 1/2 cups organic sugar
  • 1 cup milk chocolate chips
  • 1 cup organic butter
  • 2 tbl spoons of organic vanila

Instructions

  1. Mix eggs and sugar until creamy
  2. Add vanila
  3. Slowly mix in flour a little at a time
  4. Mix in chocolate chips
  5. Bake on 350 degrees for 12 minutes
